Detailed Description

Lee distance metric.

\[d(x, y) = \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} \min\{|x_i - y_i|, m - |x_i - y_i|\},\]

where \(m\) is the alphabet size, and \(x\) and \(y\) are of the same length. When \(m = 2\) or \(m = 3\), LeeDistance is the same as HammingDistance.
